Our Journey

A look at the milestones that have shaped our company's history.

November 2024

Foundation and First Products

EcoProw is founded in Roodepoort, Johannesburg with a mission to revolutionize the tile adhesives market in South Africa. Our flagship products, ECOLAIN 18 and ECORAMIC 24, are launched to serve professional contractors.

2025

Sustainable Initiative Launch

Committed to our environmental goals, we introduce a new line of eco-friendly products and reduce our carbon footprint in manufacturing processes.
